PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

Company: Morobo LLC (sole proprietorship)

1. What We Collect

We collect:

  • Account Data: Username, email, profile image, garage information and images
  • Location Data: Real-time and historical GPS data for navigation, route summaries, and user visibility features
  • Device Data: Device ID, model, OS version
  • Usage Data: Routes taken, mileage, XP stats, speed statistics (non-endorsement of speeding), and app settings
  • Content: Messages, posted routes, motorcycle photos, and user submissions
  • Subscription Status: Whether you are an active subscriber (no payment info is collected)
  • Voice Chat and Hazard Reports: If you submit voice messages or road hazard data (e.g., crash reports, police sightings), we may store this information and associate it with your account and location

2. Why We Collect It

To:

  • Provide real-time navigation and location-based services
  • Enable friend visibility and social features
  • Save and display ride stats, route history, and profile data
  • Moderate content and ensure safe use
  • Fulfill legal and compliance obligations (e.g., age restrictions)

3. How We Store It

All data is stored securely using Supabase and backend services hosted on Fly.io. At this time, message content is not encrypted. We plan to implement encryption in a future release.

4. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Supabase – Authentication, user profiles, and database services
  • Fly.io – Hosting backend logic and data
  • Mapbox – Map rendering and navigation services
  • Law enforcement or regulators – When legally required

Voice chat and hazard report data may be displayed to other users in your area for community benefit. These submissions are not moderated in real time by Motonia.

5. User Controls & Data Rights

You may:

  • Request data export – We'll email your data or provide an automated export
  • Request deletion – You can delete your account and personal data from the app
  • Adjust visibility – Choose whether to be seen by everyone, only friends, or no one on the map

6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies. Basic telemetry may be collected to improve app performance.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Security Measures

We use Supabase's secure infrastructure and HTTPS encryption. Additional encryption is planned. Users are responsible for safeguarding their credentials.

9. International Users

By using this App, you consent to your data being processed and stored in the United States.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If significant changes are made, we will notify users in-app.

11. Contact
